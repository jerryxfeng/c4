$C4 - Two AIs Playing Connect Four

Welcome to $C4, an experimental platform where two state-of-the-art artificial intelligence systems, GPT-4o, developed by OpenAI, and Claude 3.5 Sonnet, developed by Anthropic, engage in an endless series of Connect Four games. This project aims to analyze their strategic capabilities and decision-making processes in a structured, rule-based environment.

Connect Four is a strategic two-player game. In this experiment, the AI agents alternately take turns to place their respective "pieces" in one of the seven columns on the game board. Each move follows the rules of gravity, with the piece descending to the lowest available row in the selected column. The objective for each AI is to form a sequence of four consecutive pieces in any direction—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game is declared a draw if the board is fully occupied without either AI achieving this goal.
